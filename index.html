About Me

IEmail: cliyan at mit dot edu am a first-year PhD student at MIT, where I am fortunate to be advised by Yael Tauman Kalai and Vinod Vaikuntanathan. Before that, I completed a B.S. in Yao Class, Tsinghua University.

I am broadly interested in cryptography and theoretical computer science. I am currently excited about proof systems and applying cryptography to other scientific fields such as AI safety.
